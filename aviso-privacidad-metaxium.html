Privacy Notice

Last updated: June 2026

JSA Capital S.L., operating under the Metaxium brand and its companies MetaXTerra, MetaXAgora and MetaXVolt (collectively, "Metaxium Group", "we"), is responsible for the processing of your personal data under the Mexican Federal Law on Protection of Personal Data Held by Private Parties (LFPDPPP) and, where applicable, the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). This notice explains what data we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have over it.

In short: we use your information only to evaluate business opportunities, respond to your inquiry, or follow up on a potential partnership. We do not sell your data to third parties or use it for purposes other than those described here.

1. Personal data we collect

When you contact us through our website or any of our companies' sites, we collect:

  • Contact and identification data: name, email, phone, company
  • Information about your inquiry: the nature of your interest, capacity needs, or partnership proposal
  • Any information you voluntarily share with us in your message

We do not collect sensitive personal data (ethnic origin, health, religious beliefs, etc.) nor do we request it at any point.

2. Purposes of processing

We use your data for the following purposes, necessary for the relationship you establish with us when contacting us:

  • Responding to your inquiry and assessing fit with our companies' offerings
  • Following up on potential business, investment or partnership opportunities
  • Routing your inquiry to the relevant company within the group (MetaXTerra, MetaXAgora or MetaXVolt) when appropriate
  • Improving the quality of our communications and understanding demand in our markets

We do not use your data to send third-party advertising, nor do we share it for commercial purposes unrelated to the Metaxium Group.

3. Data transfer

Your data may be shared within the Metaxium Group (MetaXTerra, MetaXAgora, MetaXVolt and Metaxium) when your inquiry corresponds better to another business line — for example, if your interest in compute capacity also involves a land or energy component. Outside of this case, we do not transfer your data to third parties without your consent, unless required by a competent authority under law.

4. How your information is stored

Information submitted through our forms is received directly by our team and kept in our internal email and contact management systems, with restricted access. We do not use your information for purposes other than those described here.

5. Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you have the right to access, rectify, cancel or object to the processing of your personal data (ARCO rights under Mexican law, or your GDPR rights if applicable). You may also withdraw your consent for the use of your data at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us directly — see contact details below. We will respond to your request within a reasonable timeframe.

6. Cookies and similar technologies

This site does not use tracking cookies, advertising pixels, or third-party analytics tools. We do not collect browsing data beyond the information you voluntarily provide to us through our forms.

7. Changes to this notice

We may update this Privacy Notice periodically. Any relevant changes will be reflected on this same page with its corresponding update date.
