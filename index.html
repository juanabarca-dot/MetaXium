What we do

We control the full stack.

Mexico's digital infrastructure gap is structural. Demand is accelerating — nearshoring, AI adoption, data sovereignty regulation — while supply remains constrained by energy access bottlenecks and the absence of integrated operators. Metaxium is built to close that gap from the ground up.

01

Land & site control

We identify, acquire and develop strategic land with verified energy access. The site is the constraint — we solve it first.

02

Infrastructure development

We build and own the physical data center — modular, Tier II+, designed for wholesale colocation. CapEx structured as bankable project finance assets.

03

Energy & power delivery

Guaranteed power delivery in markets where grid capacity is constrained. Diesel backup, BESS integration and direct energy procurement where applicable.

04

Capacity commercialization

We lease guaranteed power capacity to enterprise clients, cloud players and colocation operators — USD contracts, 5–10 year terms, 99.9% SLA.

Why Metaxium

Four structural advantages.

This is not a technology bet. It is a structural infrastructure play in a market with documented supply constraints and accelerating institutional demand.

Land-first model

We control the real estate before we build the infrastructure. In a market where energy-ready land is the binding constraint, that sequence matters.

Energy as the product

Our clients don't buy rack space — they buy guaranteed power delivery. In markets where grid capacity is constrained, that guarantee commands a premium.

Bankable from day one

SPV-based structure, USD-denominated contracts, non-recourse project finance. Every asset is designed to attract institutional debt from the outset.

Replicable playbook

Standardized site design, contracts and operations. The first project is the template. Each subsequent deployment is faster and cheaper than the last.
